Turvangles: Modular shapes for a build-it-yourself sculpture

Meet Turvangles: 16 unique interlocking shapes that allow users to playfully form versatile spatial environments.

Originally created as an interactive sculpture, Turvangles are designed with asymmetry and non-right angles to mathematically unlock a near-limitless number of construction possibilities.
