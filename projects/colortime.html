Colortime

It's blue thirty somewhere


RGB is a way of displaying colors on a computer screen using combinations of red, green, and blue. Each can have a value from 0 to 255, meaning there are 16,777,216 - that's 256^3 - possible RGB colors.

Here are 864,000 of them, appearing 10 per second over the course of a day. You'll see blue cycle every minute, green every hour, and red every 24 hours. Check back at different times and see what else you notice! Midnight is a fun one.

If you're interested in tracing my pivots back to a text-based color mixing game, check out the project on Github.
