Colortime

It's blue thirty somewhere


RGB is a way of displaying colors on a computer screen using combinations of red, green, and blue. Each can have a value from 0 to 255, meaning there are 16,777,216 - that's 256^3 - possible RGB colors.

Here are 864,000 of them, appearing 10 per second over the course of a day. You'll see blue cycle every minute, green every hour, and red every 24 hours. Watch for at least a minute to see blue change from 255 back to 0. Check back at different times and see what else you notice!

You can let it run in your browser or trace my pivots back to a text-based color mixing game on Github.
