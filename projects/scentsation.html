Scentsation

Smells wonderful

We know that smell and emotion are closely connected. It can attract or repel us, calm or exite us, and instantly evoke a distant memory. A smell may make you feel a mix of things, but what would the essence of a feeling smell like? Here are my own (subjective) interpretations of eight emotionally weighted words:


WONDER . BOREDOM . ANXIETY . PASSION . SPONTANEIETY . CONTEMPLATION . SATISFACTION . SPITE

Sadly, we can't yet smell via the internet, but try using you're mind's nose to experience these "scentsations". Then watch participants try to decipher which ingredients are in which concoctions. What are their thoughts based on?
