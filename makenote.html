Make Note: A musical instrument that records your composition

Outcome
Compose, record, perform, and edit your piece in real time by arranging physical notes with this unique musical instrument.

Inspiration

When we think "digital," we might normally think of computers, or perhaps we imagine data floating around in the cloud. But what about something that simply operates within discrete states?

I wanted to combine the benefits of physical and digital composition with an instrument that could hold its musical state while allowing interactors to physically play and manipulate their composition.

Interaction

To compose, arrange the tuned aluminum notes around the large gear. To play, turn the small gear. Pause and revise your composition at any time. You can even even play it backwards!
