Animating Era Pro


PAX Labs' latest in premium cannabis technology, Era Pro is a connected concentrate vaporizer that delivers the gold standard of quality, safety, and experience to consumers.

As the creative technologist for Era Pro, I worked closely with engineering teams and project managers to define the animation "language" of the device.

Era Pro responds to user interactions with color, movement, and haptic feedback to communicate everything from temperature and battery level to pod information.

I was responsible for writing detailed spec sheets to convey device animations and UX behavior to the firmware team. Initially, I spent many hours with engineers building a technical understanding of the device. This facilitated smooth handoff of designs, and provided knowledge for how to best use Era Pro's capabilities when designing user interactions.

Built an internal prototyping tool to precisely communicate animations both visually and technically to PMs and firmware

Wrote and designed the Era Pro User Guide, a comprehensive walk-through of device functionality and animations
