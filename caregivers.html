Designing healthcare solutions that meet the needs of caregivers

Outcome
My team at Northwestern engaged in human-centered design research to uncover ways to assist caregivers and their aging loved-ones in healthcare.

Expertise
Design research
Human-centered design

Research

We first conducted remote user interviews with caregivers, secondary research, and diary studies.

We then synthesized our findings and came up with themes, HMWs, ideas for brainstorming, and ideas to test.

We tested our initial concepts with users to get to improve our design, then proposed our final design solution.
